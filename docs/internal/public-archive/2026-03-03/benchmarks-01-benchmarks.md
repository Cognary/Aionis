## Evidence Dimensions

1. Performance: latency and error-rate under documented profiles.
2. Reliability: reproducible runs and stable outputs.
3. Controllability: policy-loop observability and decision traceability.
4. Operability: gate-friendly evidence bundles for production reviews.

## Reproducibility

Every benchmark page includes:

1. Scope statement
2. Environment parameters
3. Commands
4. Artifact paths
5. Pass/fail interpretation guidance
